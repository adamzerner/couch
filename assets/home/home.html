Tater Tot Watch videos with friends. No more "1, 2, 3 - pause".

localhost:8000/

Motivation:

  • I watch YouTube videos with long distance friends while we FaceTime.
  • To start at the same time, we have to say, "One, two, three - start", and then click play.
  • If we need to pause, we have to say, "One, two, three - pause", and then click pause.
  • It shouldn't be this way.
  • With Tater Tot, you can play/pause your friends video.

How it Works:

  1. Create a room.
  2. Send the URL to whomever you want to watch the video with.
  3. Upload a YouTube video.
  4. Watch it! Use the controls to play/pause/skip to.
